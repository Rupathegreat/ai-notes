## ⚠️ YOUTUBE LIMITATION

**Why YouTube Fails:**
- YouTube has strong anti-bot protection (403 errors)
- Affects ALL download tools (yt-dlp, youtube-dl, etc.)
- Not a bug in our app - it's YouTube's security

**Solution for YouTube:**
1. Use a YouTube downloader website (e.g., y2mate.com, savefrom.net)
2. Download the video/audio to your computer
3. Upload the file directly to Smart Notes
4. 100% success rate!
